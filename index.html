About Me

Jim Girardeau is the CTO/Founder of Uptime Solutions, Inc. Uptime Solutions is a privately funded company developing products for wireless machinery management.

Connect with me

At Uptime Solutions wwww.uptime-solutions.us Jim manage two design teams working in the area of machine learning, digital signal processing, linux programming, embedded C/C++, and a web team developing a responsive web site using node.js and electron.

Previously Jim was a the VP of Engineering of ViXS systems, and managed multiple worldwide design centers in the areas of digital processing, video processing, and analog systems, and a Principal Engineer at Intel, a sr. digital designer at Level One Technologies and Motorola, Inc.
